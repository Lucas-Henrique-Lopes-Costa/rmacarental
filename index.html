Common questions

FAQ

What documents are required to rent?
A valid driver's licence (category B or higher), a government-issued ID and a credit card for the security deposit. For businesses, we require company registration documents.
What is the minimum rental period?
The minimum rental period is 1 day. For rentals of more than 7 days, we offer special discount conditions.
What is included in the daily rate?
The daily rate includes the fully inspected vehicle, mandatory insurance, 24h roadside assistance and WhatsApp support. Full comprehensive insurance can be added separately.
Can I cancel my booking?
Yes. Cancellations made more than 48 hours in advance incur no charge. Cancellations within 48 hours may incur a fee equivalent to 1 daily rate.
How does payment work?
We accept credit card, debit card and PIX. Payment is made at the time of vehicle pick-up.
Do the vehicles have tracking?
Yes, all vehicles in our fleet are equipped with a vehicle tracking system, ensuring safety throughout the entire rental period.
